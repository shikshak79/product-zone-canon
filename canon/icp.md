# ICP - Small B-2B Development Agiencies (Sample)

decision_code: ICP-001
status: Locked
version: 1.0.0
current_answer: |
  Product.Zone best fits small to mid-market B-2B development agencies (approximately 10-75 employees)
  that already have strong client demand but inconsistent new-business conversion. They tend to rely on referrals,
  outbound not operating well, and need a clear, repeatable productized offer to grow without lowering margins.

  Signals of strong fit:
  - Founder-led sales are stalling
  - Prospects ask for clarity on outcomes, timeline, or differentiation
  - Team is busy delivering but pipeline is unpredictable
  - Leadership wants a commercial offer that can be sold consistently

  Signals of no-fit:
  - Very early agencies without client repeatability or delivery discipline
  - Large enterprise shops that need deep custom consulting more than productized positioning
  - Buyers looking only for lowest-cost execution
rationale: |
  This sample ICP captures the earliest consistent patterns from founder conversations. It is Locked for demo
  purposes so agents can compare new signals against a stable baseline.

## Supporting notes
- Primary segment: Small B-2B development agencies
- Buyer: Founder, CEO, Growth Lead
- Core pain: inconsistent pipeline from non-productized sales
- Trigger: lanching a new commercial offer or repositioning an existing one
